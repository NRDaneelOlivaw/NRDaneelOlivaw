Snip demo 引用数学公式

Simple paste the LaTeX already copied to your clipboard into your editor of choice:
$$
H(Y | X)=\sum_{x \in \mathcal{X}, y \in \mathcal{Y}} p(x, y) \log \left(\frac{p(x)}{p(x, y)}\right)
$$
上面是LaTeX源码。
